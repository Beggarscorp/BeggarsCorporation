School of Life

Giving a new life to the child beggars.

Around 1,50,000 children beg on the streets of India. But they don't want to beg. They too want to go to school like any other children.

If we don't take them to schools and give them a decent life, the country and the society will be at danger. Imagine the consequences if the anti-social elements or the terrorist organisations misuse them! They can be the easy victims of child trafficking racket, illegal organ trade, drugs racket and the likes. Chidren deserve schools,not streets. Join School of Life to ensure a better life for these unfortunate children.
