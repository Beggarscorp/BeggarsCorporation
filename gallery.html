BACKGROUND

Just before incorporation, we introduced a scheme for month-long Sawan Jalabhishek this year. 110 members subscribed to it by paying ₹ 2500.00 each. It helped us provide an alternative livelihood to two beggar families once and for all. The success of this scheme inspires us to reintroduce it as an annual program.

WHAT YOU WILL GET

It includes the following costs: akshat bilvapatra, grass leaves, fresh favorite flowers of Shiv ji to be supplied by the beggars, chandan-haldi-sindoor, unbroken rice, Ganga jal, bhog, dakshina, or fees of one priest for performing puja chanting Maha Mrityuinjay in your name, the cost of one volunteer for flawless coordination observing Monday fasting, and free food for the beggar family.
