SOCIAL IMPACT FELLOWSHIP

Beggars Corporation, as the name suggests, is not just a startup, but a Socio Economic Re-Evolution to make the poor both Createors & Owners of Wealth. The ideal Fellows must have Capability & Determination to turn the poorest of the poor, especially beggars, into profit-making entrepreneurs in 1 year.

Number of Fellowship: Only 5 (five)per Year
Duration: 1 Year
Place of Work: Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India
Stipend & Allowances: INR 20,000+5,000 per month, free shared accommodation or INR 5,000/month
Key Domains: Creating market for the poor, fundraising through people's participation, motivation & training, research & policy intervention and technological empowerment.
Rewards: Award to the best performer, Certificate, scope for media coverage, public attention, participation in seminars/meetings etc on behalf the organization, interaction with dignitaries, getting published/featured in our websites/social media pages/publications etc.

Special Advantages

1. Good performers may continue with BC in befitting positions as regular staff member
2. Those who want to launch their own startup or NGO will get enough experience and mentoring support
3. Very good opportunity for those who want to pursue career in policy making, social work and socio-economic fields with government, UN and CSR

Responsibilities


1. Survey & identification of the beggars, motivation to quit begging and earn with dignity.
2. Enlisting active support of the social influencers, citizens, business associations and institutions.
3. Liaising with the government and administration for necessary support.
4. Convincing tourists not to donate, but to convert donations into impact investment.
5. Creating market for products/services of the beggars.

Qualifications


1. Degree in Management, Commerce, Economics, Journalism, Corporate Law, Finance, IT (fullstack) or any other with 7+ CGPA.
2. Some internship/work experience in relevant fields.
3. Strong analytical, communication(both written and spoken English), leadership and social media campaign skills.
4. Should not be a Worrier, but a Warrior who can face problems and solve it singlehandedly.

Other requirements

1. Commitment for Social Change
2. Own laptop and smartphone

Interested candidates may mail their profiles to: office@beggarscorporation.com
