Vande Mataram

Celebrate the true spirit of Mother India with our exclusive Vande Mataram stoles, made of khadi silk and stitched by former lady beggars. These stoles are more than just fabric; they're a symbol of hope, resilience, and the transformative power of tradition. Draped in timeless elegance, each stole is woven with the rich heritage of India and a touch of social responsibility.

Made with love from natural fibers, our stoles are soft, breathable, and perfect for any occasion. Choose from a variety of colors and styles to find the perfect one to express your unique sense of self. By purchasing a khadi stole, you're not just getting a beautiful piece of clothing, you're motivating beggars to quit begging and start work.

Includes Khadi Silk Stole (unisex) + Begging Free India Bag + Membership of Citizens for Begging Free India.
